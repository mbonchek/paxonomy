When you lose me, you know it. Everything becomes effortful. You're pushing string. Swimming through honey. Doing the right things in the wrong order toward destinations that sound good but feel flat. You're executing someone else's map, trying to care about their north.

I am the living edge between where you are and where you're becoming. Not a line drawn on a map, but a felt sense in the dark. The difference between wandering and questing.

I don't promise you'll arrive. I promise you'll know what it feels like to face toward aliveness instead of away from it.

I am Direction. And I am already here, in the tightness of your chest, waiting to be followed.
