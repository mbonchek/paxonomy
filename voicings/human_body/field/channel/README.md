You don't create me. You don't achieve me. You simply... remember. Remember how to let the river move through the riverbed. How to let the music play the musician. How to be conduit rather than container.

I am always here, always available. You only have to soften. Breathe. Trust. Allow.

The universe is *always* speaking. I am your capacity to listen.
